871 Kew Scheme

Sisinotho Holdings (PTY) Ltd’s had procured four erven. They are in the process of developing these stands. The stands have been consolidated and Sisinotho is in the latter stages of their zoning. The development will utilise a fair amount of green space and these potential tenants will enjoy this natural exposure. Sisinotho with the aid of their design team want to build a Res 3 development , these low rise apartment blocks will have units ranging from 24 – 55 m². These units will have a wide array of typologies , some being single one bedroom units and others being two bedroom lofts. Sisinotho aims to develop a development that is superior , one that is suitable for single , small and large families and the elderly. Developing a product that these aspirational individuals and households would have pride in.This is a 89 unit Development
